Hatshepsut's Mortuary Temple

About Deir al-Bahari

This stunning terraced temple complex includes the mortuary temple of Hatshepsut, the Temple of Mentuhotep II, and the Temple of Thutmose III, all set against dramatic cliffs. The temple of Hatshepsut is one of the most beautiful and well-preserved temples in Egypt, featuring colonnaded terraces and reliefs depicting the queen's divine birth and trading expeditions.
