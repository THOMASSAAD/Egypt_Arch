About Shali Village

The iconic ruins of this 13th-century fortress town made of kershef (salt and mud-brick) dominate Siwa Oasis. The labyrinthine structures were inhabited until the 20th century when heavy rains caused damage. The site offers panoramic views of the oasis and insight into the unique Berber culture of Siwa.
